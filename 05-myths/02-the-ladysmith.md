The Ladysmith

The Ladysmith lives in woods inhabited mostly by isolated Quiet, working his craft while surrounded by natural splendor, making repairs, alterations, custom builds, whatever you need. His location is hidden, but those who need his services can hear his hammering at the anvil for miles around. These few may follow the sound to his forge - if they so desire and can pay his price.

He will remake you into anything you want, as long as you bring him the parts.
